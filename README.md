# WhereAbouts - Intelligent Location Sharing

**WhereAbouts** transforms passive location tracking into proactive family safety. While existing apps (Find My, Google Maps sharing) simply display a dot on a map, WhereAbouts adds intelligent alerts, battery monitoring, arrival notifications, and contextual insights — giving families the peace of mind they deserve.

## Why WhereAbouts?

- **Smart Alerts**: Get notified when loved ones arrive at important locations or leave designated zones
- **Battery Awareness**: Stay informed about device battery levels before they run out
- **Arrival Notifications**: Know the moment your family reaches home, work, or school
- **Contextual Intelligence**: Beyond coordinates — understand location context and patterns
- **Privacy-First Design**: Control what you share and with whom
- **Real-time Updates**: Instant location sync for those who matter most

Perfect for families, groups of friends, and anyone who values connection without compromising privacy.
